COMMERCIAL & INDUSTRIAL ENERGY LEADER

RD Future Power Ltd, a proud new venture of the prestigious RD Groups (Erode, Tamil Nadu), is dedicated to establishing India as a leader in renewable energy.

Our core focus is on Bulk Power Supply to large Corporates, Government Entities, and International Buyers. With 150 acres of dedicated solar land near Dharapuram, Tamil Nadu, we are building a state-of-the-art facility planned for an annual generation capacity of 25 MW.

We are not just generating power; we are forging long-term, stable power purchase agreements (PPAs), ensuring reliable, clean, and sustainable energy for high-demand industrial and commercial consumption. Our current negotiations include supplying power to the Bangladesh Government, highlighting our global ambition and scale.

Dharapuram Solar Park (Flagship Project)

The cornerstone of RD Future Power, this large-scale solar project is strategically located near Dharapuram, Tamil Nadu, a region chosen for its excellent solar irradiance and proximity to critical power evacuation points.

The entire development spans 150 acres of contiguous, owned land, ensuring long-term operational stability and expansion potential. This project is optimized specifically for Bulk Power Supply to fulfill the continuous, high-demand needs of large industrial and corporate clients, as well as governmental entities both domestic and international.
